# Acme Commerce Refund Policy

## Standard refund windows

| Window | Eligibility | Refund method |
|--------|-------------|---------------|
| 0-30 days post-delivery | Full refund | Original payment method |
| 31-60 days post-delivery | Partial refund (80%) | Store credit only |
| 60+ days post-delivery | Not eligible | N/A |

## Defective items

Defective items are exempt from the time window. Customers must provide:
- Order ID
- Description of the defect
- Photo evidence (optional but recommended)

Defective refunds include the original shipping cost.

## Timelines

Refunds are processed within:
- **Credit/debit card**: 5-10 business days
- **Store credit**: immediate
- **PayPal/digital wallet**: 3-5 business days

## Subscriptions

For subscription orders:
- Cancel future charges immediately upon request.
- Refund the current billing cycle only if the request is within 7 days of the charge date.
- No refunds for prior billing cycles.

## Non-refundable items

The following categories are non-refundable:
- Personalized/custom items
- Digital downloads (after access)
- Gift cards
- Clearance items marked "final sale"
