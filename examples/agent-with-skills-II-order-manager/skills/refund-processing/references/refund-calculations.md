# Refund Calculations

## Full refund (within 30 days of delivery)

- Refund = item price + proportional tax
- Shipping is **non-refundable** unless the item is defective
- Refund method: original payment method

## Partial refund (31-60 days post-delivery)

- Refund = 80% of original item price
- Refund method: store credit only
- Tax is not refunded on partial refunds

## Defective item refund (no time limit)

- Refund = item price + proportional tax + original shipping cost
- Refund method: original payment method
- No time limit applies — defective items are always eligible

## Multiple items in one order

- Calculate each item's refund separately
- Some items may be eligible while others are not
- Shipping is only included once, and only if at least one item is defective

## Proportional tax calculation

- Tax per item = (item price / order subtotal) * total tax
- Round to two decimal places
